Subscribeme

Get an RSS feed of your Twitter timeline.

Note: The feed you get back is unauthenticated (so you can use it in Google Reader, for example). However, this means that anyone with the URL generated by this service can read your Twitter timeline (tweets posted by people you follow). It WILL NOT expose direct messages or any other non-public information, but don't share the URL if you don't want someone else following all the people you follow.

Note: You can configure or remove Subscribeme's access to your Twitter account from https://twitter.com/settings/applications
